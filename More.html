Presenting Three important Character in Doraemon's Life

Nobita-Nobi

Nobita Nobi is a lovable underdog known for his academic struggles, clumsiness, and big heart. Despite his flaws, his kindness and perseverance make him an endearing character.
Doraemon is a robotic cat sent from the future to help Nobita navigate life's challenges and improve his future.

Shizuka Minamoto


Shizuka Minamoto is a kind-hearted, intelligent, and compassionate girl, often serving as the moral compass and supportive friend in the "Doraemon" series. She is admired by her peers, particularly Nobita, who has a deep affection for her.
Doraemon is Nobita's guardian and best friend, sent from the future to help improve Nobita's life and ensure a better future for his descendants.



Dorami


Dorami is Doraemon's younger sister, known for her cheerful demeanor and superior abilities, often coming to aid when Doraemon is unable to help. She is a reliable and resourceful character who occasionally steps in to resolve problems that her brother cannot handle.
Doraemon and Nobita share a deep bond where Doraemon acts as a guardian and mentor, helping Nobita navigate his challenges and improve his future.
